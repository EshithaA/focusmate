About FLOW STATE

FLOW STATE is a focused study environment that blends:

  • AI Study Buddy for automatic plan generation
  • Fair, round‑robin task scheduling with a live clock
  • Customizable Pomodoro timer
  • Rewards, levels, and community leaderboard
  • Unlockable visual themes
